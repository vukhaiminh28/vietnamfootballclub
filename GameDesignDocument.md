# VIETNAM FOOTBALL CLUB 2005
### One-Page Game Design Document

---

**Game Name:** Vietnam Football Club 2005
**Genre:** Retro 8-Bit Soccer Arcade

**Description:**
A retro football game inspired by Vietnamese PC football games and Flash football games from 2000–2005. Players pick a club, step onto a pixelated pitch, and battle a CPU opponent in a fast, arcade-style 60-second match — capturing the nostalgic feel of football games once sold on CD/DVD in Vietnamese computer shops.

---

### Objective
Score more goals than the AI-controlled opponent before the 60-second match timer runs out. Simple, immediate, arcade-style fun — no leagues, no transfers, just kickoff to final whistle.

### Rules
1. The player selects a team before the match starts.
2. Arrow keys move the player.
3. Spacebar kicks the ball.
4. Every goal awards one point.
5. The team with the most goals after 60 seconds wins.

---

### Controls
| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| Left Arrow | Move Left |
| Right Arrow | Move Right |
| Up Arrow | Jump |
| Spacebar | Kick Ball |

---

### Target Audience
Casual gamers and nostalgic players who grew up with early-2000s Vietnamese PC football titles and browser/Flash sports games. Appeals to retro gaming enthusiasts and anyone who enjoys quick, pick-up-and-play arcade sports action.

### Technology Used
Built entirely with HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript (Canvas API for rendering). No external libraries, frameworks, or assets required — a single self-contained `.html` file runs directly in any modern browser.

### Visual Style
Chunky pixel-art sprites, a flat side-view pitch, low-resolution textures, and bold scanline/CRT-style screen effects to emulate playing on an old CRT monitor. Menus use blocky retro fonts, bright saturated colors, and simple geometric UI panels — evoking the box art and in-game menus of budget football games sold on CDs in Vietnam circa 2005.
